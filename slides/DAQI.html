Air pollution
regulation


Five pollutants specified in the daily air quality index (DAQI):

Nitrogen Dioxide(N02)
Sulphur Dioxide
Ozone
Particles < 2.5µm (PM2.5)
Particles < 10µm (PM10)

In the UK, grades are set from 1 to 10 and these five substances
are intensively managed to improve air quality.
